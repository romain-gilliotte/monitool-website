Monitool

Your tool for logical frameworks data management indicators planning decision making sharing

What is it?

Monitool is a platform to host, organize and share logical frameworks for humanitarian organizations.

Instead of being "Yet Another Form Builder v2.0 ®", it heavily focuses on helping projects get their monitoring and data management planning right.

  • Which indicators will you use?
  • Do you have access to data-sources that will enable you to measure them? How often? With which level of detail?
  • Once you start measuring them, which figures do you expect to find?
  • How much of your operative staff time are you willing to allocate to data collection? Who is reponsible for what?
  • Will the effects of your project to be able to change those indicators in a reasonable timeframe?
  • Which correctives actions on your project did you plan in advance for each indicator?

Those are the questions that we hope that using Monitool will help you to answer, write down in a tidy manner and share with your team and staff.

Oh, and also, we did make a form builder.

How is it different

Forgiving

All user actions are dated, attributed to someone and can be reverted in one click. No loss of data is possible.

Progressive

No need for a revolution. Continue with paper, Excel or emails and still make your system more efficient.

Fast to deploy

No need to talk to your legal department. Only aggregated data is used, which GDPR does not cover.

Evolutive

Tool is extremely tolerant to changes in your project. Already collected data will be updated in simple and predictible ways when that happen.

Clear scope

Monitool does not try to do everything. It does one thing right, and does not get in the way of the tools you are already using.

No customer lock-in

We won't hold your data. Everything that is on the platform can be exported to Excel and other formats with the push of a button

In-app documentation

On each page a help panel is available, and contains each questions which was ever asked to us.

International

Use it in your own language

Learn by example

Samples projects which use all features of the tool are provided and will be waiting for you when you log in.

Free and Open-Source
